Abstract and Highlight Paper submission

Abstract submitted to the conference should be formatted properly. The template for abstract submission can be downloaded from Abstract Template.

 

The abstract and Highlight Paper must be uploaded through EasyChair Login Page for ICIBM'15.


Formatting of papers

All accepted papers of registered authors will be included in the special issues including:

  • BMC Genomics      click here for BMC intructions for authors
  • BMC System Biology      click here for BMC intructions for authors
  • International Journal of Computational Biology and Drug Design (IJCBDD)
    •

    Authors need to prepare manuscripts in the format of the corresponding journal that they prefer (you can find format requirements of different journals in the journal websites listed above). When submitting a paper, authors will be asked to select preferred journals. Although we try our best to accommodate authors’ preferences, there is no guarantee that every accepted paper will be published in authors’ preferred journals. We will contact individual authors in that case.

    Paper submission

    Papers will be submitted through EasyChair Conference System. Prospective authors are invited to submit unpublished work to ICIBM 2015. All papers, abstracts and highlight papers will be submitted through EasyChair Conference System. Detailed information is available through: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icibm20150. Please combine all the documents, including main text, figures, tables, and supplementary materials, into one PDF file during the submission process.
