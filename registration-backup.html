Welcome to ICIBM 2014 Registration System

                Each accepted paper must have a registration in order to be include in the final conference program.
                The deadline for early registration is Oct. 22, 2014.
                The early registration fee is $300 for student/postdoc and $400 for faculty/staff.
                After Oct. 22, the registration fee will be $350 for student/postdoc and $450 for faculty/staff.



                Hotel booking from ICIBM is available. ICIBM 2014 will take place at Sheraton Gunter Hotel.
                The attendee can book the hotel at the ICIBM’14 group rate ($149/night) from https://www.starwoodmeeting.com/Book/ICIBM
